CalcU

Welcome to Calcu! Your very own online simple calculator for two numbers! Input two numbers on each of the boxes below. After inputting two numbers, click any of the five buttons to perform the operation shown. In subtraction, the left box is for the subtrahend and the right box is for the minuend. In division and modulo, the left box is for the dividend and the right box is for the divisor.

Results will be displayed here.
